CartoDB Ocean Lights Basemap

A dark global basemap with "lights" in the oceans

This basemap exemplifies what John Pickles (2004) defined as a cartographic gaze, a miniature, yet global view of the world designed for a specific, albeit limited purpose. This basemap shows the entire world at once. However, polar areas appear distored and minimized. This basemap also prioritizes the oceans over land features, showing white "lights" in the ocean ridges. It provides an artistic approach, but has limited use. For best results, use this basemap to show generalized, global data, and avoid displaying features which demand detail as well as features that would cover up the characteristic design in the oceans.
