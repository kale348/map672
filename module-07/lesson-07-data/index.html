Lesson 7 Examples

Iguana Restaurants in Salt Lake City

Map authored by Lauren Winkler

This example displays a layer group of two circles, one red circle centered at the Red Iguana Restaurant with a 400 meter radius, and one blue circle centered at the Blue Iguana Restaurant with a 300 meter radius. Hovering over each circle changes its color to yellow. Hovering off of each circle restores it to its original color (red or blue). Earlier examples from lesson 7 are commented out.
