Major Sources of Clean Electricity in the US

Map authored by Lauren Winkler

This map displays major sources of clean electricity, specifically hydroelectric, wind, solar, geothermal, biomass, and wood power plants which produce more than 500 MW hours of power. Data is filtered from a set of 6900 electrical generating power plants across the US obtain from the EIA's form 923.
